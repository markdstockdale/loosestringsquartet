About Us

Based in Holywood, County Down, Loose Strings Quartet provides beautiful live music for weddings, ceremonies, receptions and special occasions throughout Northern Ireland. We combine classical elegance with contemporary favourites to create unforgettable moments for every event.

Wedding Music

Ceremony Music

Guest arrival, bridal entrance, signing of the register and recessional music tailored to your day.

Drinks Reception

Elegant background music while guests relax and celebrate after the ceremony.

Wedding Breakfast

Sophisticated live music to accompany your wedding meal and create a memorable atmosphere.
