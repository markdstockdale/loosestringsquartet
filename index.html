About Us

Loose Strings Quartet is a professional wedding string quartet based in Holywood, County Down. We believe music should do more than fill a space — it should shape emotion, create atmosphere, and make moments feel timeless.


From intimate church ceremonies to elegant country estates and modern venues across Northern Ireland, we provide carefully arranged live string music designed to make your wedding feel deeply personal and unforgettable.

Making Your Wedding Day Special

Every wedding has its own rhythm. We tailor our music to match each part of your day, creating a seamless emotional journey for you and your guests.

The Ceremony

The moment everything begins. As your guests take their seats, soft live strings set the tone — calm, elegant, and full of anticipation.

The Entrance

Your walk down the aisle is one of the most powerful moments of your life. We shape that moment with music chosen entirely around you.

The Celebration

From confetti moments to drinks receptions, we bring warmth, joy, and sophistication to your celebration.
