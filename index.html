About Us

Loose Strings Quartet is a professional wedding string ensemble based in Holywood, County Down, performing throughout Northern Ireland. We specialise in creating refined, live musical experiences that feel effortless, emotional, and deeply connected to your day.


Our musicians bring years of classical training and performance experience, combined with a passion for modern wedding music. We believe the best performances are never intrusive — they are felt, not forced, enhancing every moment without overwhelming it.

Your Wedding, Beautifully Scored

Every wedding tells a different story. Our role is to provide the soundtrack — subtle, elegant, and perfectly matched to your atmosphere.

The Ceremony

Soft string music as your guests arrive sets a calm and elegant tone. Each note is chosen to reflect the emotion of the moment and the importance of what is about to unfold.

The Bridal Entrance

One of the most meaningful moments of your life deserves music that feels entirely your own. We help you choose a piece that reflects your story and enhances the moment without distraction.

The Celebration

As you step into married life, live music continues through drinks receptions and gatherings, adding warmth, elegance, and a sense of effortless celebration.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can we choose our own music?

Yes. We work closely with every couple to select music that reflects your personal style and story.

How far do you travel?

We perform across all of Northern Ireland including Belfast, County Down, County Antrim and surrounding areas.

Do you perform outdoors?

Yes, provided suitable shelter and weather conditions are in place to protect instruments.

How long do you typically play for?

Performances vary depending on your package, from ceremony-only sets to several hours across your wedding day.

Do you need anything provided?

We require four armless chairs and a sheltered, level performance space.
